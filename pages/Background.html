My Style

As a composer I have always been most influenced by the
works of Beethoven, Chopin, and Nobuo Uematsu. I knew
from the second I heard the crystal prelude from one of the
final fantasy games that I knew I wanted to be a composer. I
take a firm stance of taking what the old composers and new
composers have created and create a fusion of the two.
